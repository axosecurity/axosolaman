"Ignorance and the absence of will are the root causes of all problems"

- Axosolaman

Published by Axosolaman Philosophy & Action

This quote identifies a dual failure as the source of human struggle: the lack of knowledge (ignorance) and the lack of determination (absence of will).

Knowledge alone is not enough; one must have the will to act upon it. Conversely, will without knowledge is blind and often destructive. True progress occurs at the intersection of a clear mind and a resolute spirit. In the context of security, ignorance of vulnerabilities is dangerous, but the absence of will to fix them is fatal.

"To know is to be half-armed; to will is to be fully armed."
