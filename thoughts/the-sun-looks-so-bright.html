"The sun looks so bright, but you don't know what is happening inside."

- Axosolaman

Published by Axo Solaman Philosophy & Quotes

This quote explores the hidden reality behind success and brilliance. While the world sees the radiant glow of achievement, they often remain unaware of the intense heat, pressure, and internal turmoil that sustain that light.

In the world of cybersecurity, this resonates with the life of a researcher. The successful exploit or the secured system is the "bright" exterior, but the "inside" is filled with countless hours of frustration, failed attempts, and mental exhaustion that the public never witnesses.
