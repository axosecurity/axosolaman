The Security Odyssey

My fascination with the terminal began in 2016 when I first discovered Kali Linux and Termux. While academic pressures initially kept me away, the post-COVID era marked a definitive turning point.

During my first year of college, I secured my first bounty from Coinbase. This was followed by success in several private programs, earning approximately $1,240 in my early college life. Now, having completed my HSC, I am channeling my entire energy into the world of cybersecurity.
